Research

Research interests

I work on string theory and quantum gravity, combining explicit string constructions with general consistency arguments. My research runs along two main threads.

Moduli stabilization

My work on this front has focused on building reliable 4d vacua in non-geometric LG models, scale-separated 3d and 4d AdS vacua, and 4d vacua from compactifications of type II on group manifolds.

Effective field theories

Pinning down which low-energy effective theories are compatible with quantum gravity and causality — for instance, deriving causality bounds from charged shockwaves in five dimensions, and characterising the scalar potentials and asymptotic dark-energy behaviour that the Swampland allows.
